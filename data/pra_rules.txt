PRA Own Funds Rule 1:
Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) capital includes ordinary share capital and retained earnings.

PRA Own Funds Rule 2:
Tier 1 capital consists of CET1 capital plus additional Tier 1 instruments.

PRA Own Funds Rule 3:
Total capital consists of Tier 1 capital and Tier 2 capital.

PRA Own Funds Rule 4:
Distributions such as dividends reduce CET1 capital.
